About Us

Dear Friends:

Ragehsree Music Institute was founded on 1991 to help preserve, present and further the ongoing and timeless growth on Indian Classical Music. It is our aim to help foster these magnificent art forms to their greatest capacity without sacrificing integrity in the very receptive American Culture. Rageshree Music Institute aims to presents this great art forms in America through sponsoring live concerts, education through group and individual instruction on the various instruments and voice and making recorded music available. Rageshree Music Institute is a not-for-profit organization and is dependent on the proceeds from sales of CDs and Cassettes, and also upon the contribution of the members of the institute. We thank you in advance for your support of this art and the Rageshree Music Institute. We look forward to bringing you Indian Classical Music of the highest quality and integrity.
