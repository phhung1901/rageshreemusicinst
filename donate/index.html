Donations of any amounts are welcome and much appreciated, you may do so either by check, money order or credit card (please use PayPal) to Rageshree Music Institute Inc. Upon received donation, you will receive a receipt from us which may be Tax deductable (please consult with your tax advisor). If you wish to donate $20.00 you will receive a free CD from our catalog by mail.  In addition you will also receive discount on the concert tickets for you and your friends as well. We will only use your donation toward the sponsor of a concert in the tri-state area. This is where you can make a huge difference. We thank you in advance for your continued support.

Donate $25.00 and receive any 3 CDs or DVD including S&H within USA only.

Donate $10.00 and receive one (1) CD or DVD including S&H within USA only.

Donate $25.00 and receive any 2 CDs or DVD including S&H to any international countries only.
